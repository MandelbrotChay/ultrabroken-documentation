# Out of Bounds
_Basic stub_

## Introduction
---
Out-of-bounds (OOB) refers to techniques that deliberately move the player or objects outside the intended playable area by exploiting collision, geometry, physics, or game state transitions. Common causes include clipping through walls, ledge/ledge-cancel exploits, menu or animation interrupts, map transitions, and interactions with special effects (lasers, zuggles, Moobe/menu overload). OOB is used to reach inaccessible zones, skip content, retrieve items, or trigger unusual world states; it can break scripts or cause forced repositions.
