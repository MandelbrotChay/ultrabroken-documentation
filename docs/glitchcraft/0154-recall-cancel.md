# Recall Cancel
_R4000 - 20 July 2023_

## Overview
Lets you delay the dropping of weapon and items

`1.2.0` `1.2.1`

## Instructions
1. Hold L and go to recall
2. Drop a weapon, bow, shield, or item from the quick menu before the rune activates

## Notes
Jumpslash cancel
