# Travel Medallion storage `TMS`

## Overview
Places the travel medallion at links feet after loading a file.

## Instructions
1. Go to 0295 -1552 1364
2. Hit the broken raft (anything will work) and hit + right before the your hit makes contact with the raft (it also work with koroks in a ballon but it is much harder)
3. Go watch 1 to 5 memories (it depends of when you hit +, 3 and 1 are the most common numbers)
4. Then teleport to a travel medallion (take one you don't really care about)
5. The teleportation should be interupted by the cutscene
6. Now the frist time you get a loading screen the medallion will go under link feet at the end of the loading screen
